About Us

Your Partner In Peak Performance

Founded on the principles of excellence and dedication, Lift Fitness has become the premier fitness destination for those who demand the best. With two strategic locations in Kottawa and Rathmalana, we have spent over 10 years perfecting the art of physical transformation.

Our mission is simple: To provide an elite fitness environment that guarantees results. By merging a luxury club atmosphere with result-driven training programs, we ensure that every member who walks through our doors is equipped to succeed.

With a growing community of 400+ active members, we continue to lead the way in Sri Lanka's fitness industry, offering world-class facilities and expert guidance to help you reach your peak performance.

Our Services

What We Offer

From personal training to group classes, we have everything you need to achieve your fitness goals.

Elite Personal Coaching

Achieve your fitness goals faster with personalized 1-on-1 coaching. Our expert trainers in Kottawa and Rathmalana design custom workout routines tailored to your body type and fitness level.

Personalized Diet & Nutrition

Fuel your body for success with expert-guided meal plans. We provide science-based nutrition coaching to complement your training, helping you stay healthy and see real progress from the inside out.

Strength & Endurance Zone

Build power and improve heart health with our state-of-the-art facilities. From heavy lifting to high-intensity cardio, we offer the best equipment to help you build a stronger, leaner physique.

90-Day Transformation Challenge

Join our signature results-driven challenges designed for total body transformation. Push your limits with a community of like-minded individuals and see life-changing results under professional guidance.
